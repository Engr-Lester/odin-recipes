Adobo Recipe

ADOBO: Simple Ingredients, Legendary Flavor

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken or pork, cut into serving pieces
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup vinegar (usually cane vinegar)
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 whole garlic, crushed
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 1-2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional, for balance)
  • Salt to taste (optional)

How to Cook

  1. Marinate: Mix the meat with soy sauce and garlic. Let it marinate for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  2. Sauté: Heat oil in a pot or pan. Add the garlic and sauté until fragrant. Add the marinated chicken and cook for 3-5 minutes until lightly browned.
  3. Simmer: Pour in: marinade (soy sauce mixture), vinegar, water, Add bay leaves and peppercorns. Do not stir immediately after adding vinegar (to keep flavor balanced). Bring to a boil.
  4. Cook: Lower heat and simmer for 25–30 minutes until chicken is tender. Stir occasionally. Add sugar if you want slightly sweeter adobo.
  5. Reduce the Sauce: Simmer uncovered for 5-10 minutes until sauce thickens.
    Tips:
  • For richer flavor, some people fry the chicken first before simmering.
  • You can also make pork adobo or mix pork and chicken.
  • Adobo actually tastes better the next day because the flavors soak in.
